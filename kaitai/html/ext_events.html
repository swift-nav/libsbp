ExtEvents format specification

Type: ExtEvents

OffsetSizeIDTypeNote

Type: MsgExtEvent

Reports detection of an external event, the GPS time it occurred, which pin it was and whether it was rising or falling.

OffsetSizeIDTypeNote
0 ... wn u2le GPS week number
2 ... tow u4le GPS time of week rounded to the nearest millisecond
6 ... ns_residual s4le Nanosecond residual of millisecond-rounded TOW (ranges from -500000 to 500000)
10 ... flags u1 Flags
11 ... pin u1 Pin number. 0..9 = DEBUG0..9.
