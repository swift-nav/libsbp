Type: MsgSsrFlagHighLevel

Integrity monitoring flags for multiple aggregated elements. An element could be a satellite, SSR grid point, or SSR tile. A group of aggregated elements being monitored for integrity could refer to: - Satellites in a particular {GPS, GAL, BDS} constellation. - Satellites in the line-of-sight of a particular SSR tile. - Satellites in the line-of-sight of a particular SSR grid point. The integrity usage for a group of aggregated elements varies according to the integrity flag of the satellites comprising that group. SSR_INTEGRITY_USAGE_NOMINAL: All satellites received passed the integrity check and have flag INTEGRITY_FLAG_OK. SSR_INTEGRITY_USAGE_WARNING: A limited number of elements in the group failed the integrity check. Refer to more granular integrity messages for details on the specific failing elements. SSR_INTEGRITY_USAGE_ALERT: Most elements in the group failed the integrity check, do not use for positioning. SSR_INTEGRITY_USAGE_NOT_MONITORED: Unable to verify the integrity flag of elements in the group.
