Raw data from the magnetometer.

OffsetSizeIDTypeNote
0 ... tow u4le Milliseconds since start of GPS week. If the high bit is set, the time is unknown or invalid.
4 ... tow_f u1 Milliseconds since start of GPS week, fractional part
5 ... mag_x s2le Magnetic field in the body frame X axis
7 ... mag_y s2le Magnetic field in the body frame Y axis
9 ... mag_z s2le Magnetic field in the body frame Z axis
